4 Highlights in Hillsborough, Co. Down

Here are four must-see spots: Hillsborough Castle & Gardens, St Malachy’s Church, Hillsborough Forest Park and the world famous Hillside Pub and Restaurant. Tap the buttons for official info, maps, and planning.

Hillsborough Castle & Gardens

Royal residence Gardens Tours

A historic royal residence with beautifully maintained gardens and seasonal events. Ideal for a half-day visit with cafés and gift shop on-site.

Listen:
  • Best for: history lovers, gardens, photography
  • Allow: 2–4 hours (longer if you explore the grounds)

St Malachy’s Church, Hillsborough

Heritage Architecture Quiet stop

A peaceful and historic church in the village—great for a quiet moment and a short heritage stop while exploring Hillsborough’s streets and The Square.

Listen:
  • Best for: local history, architecture, calm atmosphere
  • Allow: 20–45 minutes (more if you stroll the village)

Hillsborough Forest Park

Woodland trails Lakes Family friendly

A brilliant spot for fresh air, woodland walks and lakeside views. Perfect if you want an easy nature break close to the village.

  • Best for: walking, picnics, dog walks, relaxed outdoors
  • Allow: 1–3 hours depending on the route

The Hillside Pub

Historic pub Food & drink Village landmark

Hillsborough’s oldest pub, established in 1752. The Hillside is a much-loved village institution, known for its welcoming atmosphere, traditional character, and quality food and drink.

  • Best for: lunch, dinner, local pints, relaxed evenings
  • Location: heart of Hillsborough village
